## 1. Diagnosis

### Why Day 1 Matters
Day 1 is the first real contact between Mutirão de Saneamento and actual players. It validates:
- Whether the T76/T77/T78 preparation actually works
- Whether players understand the game
- Whether mobile playability is viable
- Whether the sprint should continue, pause, or pivot

**This is NOT the beta decision.** This is the operational decision to continue the sprint.

### Risks Being Mitigated
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Theory vs reality gap | Real player feedback |
| Unknown blockers | Early discovery before 8 sessions |
| Mobile viability failure | Mandatory mobile validation |
| Data pipeline failure | End-to-end export verification |
| Wasted effort | Go/No-Go after 3 sessions, not 8 |
