PHYSICS SYSTEM VALIDATION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Core Engine (cannon-board.ts):
  • Cannon.js world setup with standard gravity (9.82 m/s²)
  • Friction: 0.3 (controlled slides, not slippery)
  • Restitution: 0.4 (slight bounce, feels solid)
  • Ground reference for physics anchoring
  • Impact listener pattern for event handling

Blockage Design (concrete-barrier.ts):
  • 2×2 grid of individual physics bodies (pieces)
  • Health system: 0–100 integrity
  • Damage scaling: 15G threshold (whisper), 35G breakage threshold
  • Piece separation: High force causes independent piece physics
  • Cascade potential: Falling pieces can hit other pieces

Tool Design (rammer-tool.ts):
  • Mass: 80kg (feels weighty, not trivial)
  • Base velocity: 25 m/s (scales with power slider 0–1)
  • Physics-driven flight: Gravity applied, spin on launch
  • Trajectory preview: Parabolic arc calculation
  • Impact calculation: Force = mass × velocity
