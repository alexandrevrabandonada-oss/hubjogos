## 1. Diagnosis: Why First Art Production Now

### The Risk Being Addressed
T93 concept and T94 art direction are locked. The next bottleneck is **proving the lane visually**:
- Can this look like a real game, not just a concept?
- Does the art support movement readability?
- Will screenshots sell the territorial atmosphere?

### The Goal
Produce the minimum art package that proves visual identity:
- Protagonist character sheet (readable at scale)
- Tile/prop language (distinctive, authentic)
- Screenshot mockups (shareable, compelling)

### Success Criteria
| Criterion | Test |
|-----------|------|
| Character readability | Silhouette clear at 48px on mobile |
| Tile distinctiveness | Laje ≠ telha ≠ escada at glance |
| Prop authenticity | Caixa d'água reads as "favela Rio" instantly |
| Screenshot power | Someone asks "what game is this?" |
| Readability preserved | Can still play with this art |
