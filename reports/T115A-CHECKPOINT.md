## Four Early Signal Metrics

### 1. Retry Rate (Primary Growth Signal)
**Definition:** % of testers who say "Yes/Maybe" to playing another round  
**Target:** >70%  
**Why:** Immediate replay desire = authentic toy factor, not novelty

### 2. Fun Factor (Baseline Enjoyment)
**Definition:** Average 1–10 fun rating  
**Target:** ≥7.0  
**Why:** Confirms baseline satisfaction (not just polished)

### 3. Aiming Confidence (Core Interaction)
**Definition:** Average 1–5 rating on aiming intuitiveness  
**Target:** ≥4.0  
**Why:** If players can't aim, nothing else matters

### 4. Would Share (Viral Potential)
**Definition:** % of testers who say "Yes/Maybe" to sharing with friends  
**Target:** >50%  
**Why:** Franchises need word-of-mouth potential

---

## Three Gate Evaluations

### Gate 1: Toy Factor Survival (2–3 criteria pass)
- Retry ≥70%?
- Fun ≥7.0?
- Would share ≥50%?

If 2–3 pass → Toy factor is real ✅

### Gate 2: No Critical Friction (All 3 criteria pass)
- <3 players flag same blocker?
- Aiming confidence ≥4.0?
- No "unusable on [device]" complaints?

If all pass → No blocking friction ✅

### Gate 3: Device Readiness (3–4 criteria pass)
- iOS working?
- Android working?
- Desktop working?
- Audio/haptics functional?

If 3–4 pass → Platform support solid ✅

## Decision Outcomes

| Outcome | Criteria | Action | Timeline |
|---------|----------|--------|----------|
| **A: SCALE** | All 3 gates pass | Launch full T115 testing (20–50 players) | Week 1–2 |
| **B: POLISH** | 2 gates pass + friction identified | Fix friction, re-test, then scale | 2–3 days |
| **C: PAUSE** | 0–1 gates pass OR toy <6.0 | Post-mortem analysis, strategic decision | 1 week |

## What's NOT in Scope (T115A Pilot)

- ❌ Second blockage type (Rubble, Cable, etc. = T116)
- ❌ Progression system (T116)
- ❌ Cosmetics / customization (T116)
- ❌ Multiple tools (T116+)
- ❌ Rigorous statistical study (this is *early signal*, not statistically valid)
- ❌ Deep qualitative interviews (collect feedback quickly, don't over-analyze)

**Pilot is:** Quick, lean, early-signal gate  
**Pilot is NOT:** Definitive research
